To Play EaseCation

IP & Port

IP: play.easecation.net
Port: 19132 (Default)

Device Requirements

1. iOS/Android/Windows System
2. Android4.4 and Above; iOS8 and Above; Windows 10
3. Has a stable connection to the Internet

1.Find Minecraft in the App Store

Minecraft Bedrock edition could be downloaded via App Store, Google Play or Microsoft Store.

  • App Store
  • Official Website
  • Microsoft Store
  • Google Play

2.Add EaseCation to the Server List

Click "Add Server", and type in IP & Port, then click "Save".

Enjoy!
